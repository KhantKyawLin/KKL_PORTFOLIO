About Me

Hello! I'm Khant Kyaw Lin, an adaptive and passionate Full-Stack Developer with a journey that bridges healthcare, education, and technology. I specialize in building scalable, secure, and user-focused web applications that make a real-world impact.

My path began in healthcare—earning 4 distinctions in Myanmar’s matriculation exam, studying at the University of Medicine 2, and later serving as Operations Lead at Vital Care Pharmacy. Alongside this, I dedicated nearly a decade to mentoring high school students as a freelance study guide, cultivating problem-solving and communication skills.

In 2023, I transitioned into technology, completing an NCC Education Level 4 Diploma in Computing with Distinction (79%) and currently pursuing Level 5. Since then, I’ve achieved multiple industry-recognized certifications in Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Laravel, MySQL), AI & Machine Learning (IBM, Google, Cisco), and Cybersecurity (Cyber Threat Management, Network Defense, Endpoint Security). I also completed a PHP Internship at Soft Guide Software House, where I worked on real-world projects including an E-learning platform and a Pharmacy Management System.

Beyond technical expertise, I’ve strengthened my professional foundation with courses in English for IT (CEFR B2), Digital Awareness, Docker, and Professional Communication. These experiences allow me to collaborate effectively in diverse teams while delivering both technical excellence and business value.

With a blend of leadership, adaptability, and continuous learning, I am eager to contribute to innovative teams and forward-thinking projects—combining full-stack development, AI integration, and cybersecurity principles to create intelligent and reliable digital solutions.
