About Me

"Software should be simple, useful, and above all- make someone's life easier."

Hi, I am Khant Kyaw Lin. My background isn’t just in code-it is in people. After years of managing diverse teams in healthcare and student groups, I realized that the most satisfying part of any job is building the tools that solve real, daily frustrations.

I don't just "build apps;" I help you serve your users better. Whether it's a small-scale tool or a live, complex ecosystem, here is what I can do for your next project:

  • Scalable Full-Stack Solutions: I build secure backend RESTful APIs and modern web interfaces using Laravel, Vue.js, React.js, and Bootstrap, as well as cross-platform mobile apps with Flutter.
  • Actionable Data Insights: I help you understand your audience. Recently, I integrated Google Analytics for Firebase into a live Flutter LMS app to track user engagement and drive data-backed improvements.
  • Secure & Resilient Systems: With a strong foundation in Cybersecurity (Cisco & IBM certified), I implement robust security controls like JWT authentication and Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) to protect user data from day one.
  • Simplifying Complex Workflows: I specialize in taking complicated business logic—such as multi-role approval structures, inventory management systems, and e-learning portals—and turning them into clean, automated digital platforms.

I am a developer who can build simple but useful software. If you have a project that needs a steady hand, a secure foundation, and a focus on real-world impact, let's build it together.
