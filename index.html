About Me

Hello! I'm Khant Kyaw Lin, an adaptive and passionate Full-Stack Developer with a unique journey bridging healthcare, education, and technology. I thrive on creating efficient, scalable, and user-focused web applications that deliver real-world impact.

My path began in healthcare—earning 4 distinctions in Myanmar’s matriculation exam, studying at the University of Medicine 2, and later leading operations at Vital Care Pharmacy. Alongside this, I spent nearly a decade as a freelance study guide, mentoring high school students to academic success.

In 2023, I transitioned into technology, completing a Level 4 Diploma in Computing with Distinction and currently pursuing Level 5. I have hands-on experience from a three-month internship at Soft Guide Software House, as well as skills in PHP, Laravel, MySQL, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and full-stack development. I’m now expanding into Python and artificial intelligence to build intelligent, scalable solutions.

With a blend of leadership, problem-solving, and technical expertise, I am eager to contribute to innovative teams and projects, bringing both adaptability and a relentless drive to learn.
